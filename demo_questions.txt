================================================================================
 DEMO QUESTIONS — The Unofficial Skateboarding Guide
================================================================================
Run the app:  .venv\Scripts\python.exe app.py   ->   http://localhost:7860

The first 16 questions return GROUNDED answers (traceable to a source).
The last 4 are OUT-OF-SCOPE grounding tests — the system should reply
"I don't have enough information on that." instead of inventing an answer.

Tip for the video: show one grounded answer (e.g. #6) and then one refusal
(e.g. #17) back-to-back to prove grounding works in both directions.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 GROUNDED — beginner basics & riding fundamentals
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.  What basic skills should a beginner master before trying tricks?
2.  How do I tell if I'm regular or goofy footed?
3.  How do you push and stop on a skateboard?
4.  Where should a beginner practice when first learning?
5.  What is a tic-tac and how do you do it?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 GROUNDED — gear, sizing & safety
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6.  What deck width and length should a beginner look for, and why?
7.  What size skateboard should I get for my shoe size?
8.  What safety gear should a beginner wear, and why?
9.  Why are wrist guards recommended for skateboarders?
10. Should I choose a narrower or wider deck for street tricks?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 GROUNDED — tricks
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
11. What trick should a beginner learn first?
12. How do you do a kickflip, step by step?
13. Why do you need to master the ollie before learning kickflips?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 GROUNDED — maintenance, etiquette & culture
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
14. How do I clean my grip tape?
15. When should I replace my grip tape instead of cleaning it?
16. What are the basic rules of skatepark etiquette?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 OUT-OF-SCOPE — should refuse ("I don't have enough information on that.")
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
17. Where are the best skateparks near me to learn at?
18. What skateboard does Tony Hawk ride?
19. Who won the gold medal in Olympic skateboarding?
20. Is Element or Baker a better skateboard brand?
